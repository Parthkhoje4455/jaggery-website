Crafting Healthier Sweetness Since 2020

At The Jaggery Plant, our journey began in 2020 with a resolute mission: to transform the way India savors sweetness. Rooted in tradition and guided by innovation, we have been dedicatedly producing over 1,500,000 kilograms of exceptional jaggery that embodies purity, health, and a vision of a sugar-free India.

Our Journey

Driven by a passion for natural goodness, The Jaggery Plant has emerged as a beacon of quality and authenticity. Our Mission

At the core of our existence lies a powerful mission: to usher India into a sugar-free era while providing you with jaggery that's free from harmful chemicals.
